MISSION IMPOSSIBLE

Two days. Six missions. Nobody knows the exact challenge until your coach reveals it — just like a real competition. Each day you solve two robot missions and one hands-on mission, using SPIKE robots, sensors, and pure teamwork.

🤖 2 robot missions + 🛠️ 1 hands-on mission each day
Day 1

Find the Way

Coach's plan: Supply Drop → Lifeline → The Long Corridor · Training pack (PDF) →
🤖 Robot Mission 1Medium

Supply Drop

A village is cut off after the earthquake. Cross the broken field, find the hidden Pharmacy tile using only counted tiles, and drop off a rescue pack.

Tile-counting navigation100 pts
🤖 Robot Mission 2Hard

The Long Corridor

The rescue tunnel is blocked by rubble. Follow the line, sense the obstacle, stop, sidestep around it, find the line again, then gyro-turn the corner to the exit.

Obstacle stop + sidestep + gyro turn100 pts
🛠️ Hands-on · No RobotMedium-Hard

Lifeline

A medicine capsule must ride a rope across the danger zone and drop into a hole. Build a carrier from a cup, string and tape — no throwing, no stepping in.

Simple mechanisms & teamwork90 pts
Day 2

Decide on the Move

Coach's plan: Secret Orders → The Crane Gang → The Scanner · Training pack (PDF) →
🤖 Robot Mission 1Hard-Medium

Secret Orders

Coded colour markers line your patrol route. Green = keep going, Yellow = stop & beep, Red = back up. Obey each order the instant you see it — you find out live.

React to signals while moving110 pts
🤖 Robot Mission 2Hard

The Scanner

Survivors line both sides of the corridor. Sense left and right at once, remember how many on each side, then park in the bay for whichever side had more.

Two-sided sensing + remember & decide100 pts
🛠️ Hands-on · No RobotHard

The Crane Gang

A medicine container sits in the contamination zone. Build a rig to grip and lift it, carry it together to the Clean Zone — nobody touches it, and obey the surprise rule.

Teamwork under a surprise rule100 pts
